Nutritional information on healthy products from stores

It is difficult to stick to a diet when eating outside, since there is rarely any available nutritional information on meals. With your help we can change this

Become a partner

How it works

Do you own a food store?

Create a business account
Are your healthy meals produced with a consistent quantity of each ingredient? Then come on board with us and help people know what they eat, even when outside. Submit this form to request a business account.
Add products
Upload your meals, along with their nutritional information, to our platform. If you haven’t calculated the nutritional values, you can either do that with our tool or let our nutritional analysis partner undertake the whole process.
Increase customer satisfaction
When it’s time for people to eat, they can find the right meal based on their diet, thanks to the nutritional info of your meals. You can keep track of how many customers have viewed your products and visited your store
